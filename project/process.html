Individual Social Media Posts

First, each individual group member sketched out faux social media posts to bring ideas to the table when deciding what and how to promote Hedy Lamarr.

Social Media Prototype 1

After meeting and discussing the individual posts, Team 17 discussed how to bring their plan together. They updated the sketches to create more official looking posts to then ask for feedback on through a survey.

Report can be found here.

Social Media Prototype 2

Among themselves, the members of Team 17 gave feedback on the posts and fixed things like grammar issues among other minor problems.

Report can be found here.

Survey

Using the first round of prototypes, a survey was made to ask willing participants which posts worked and which didn't for the goal the team outlined: "With these posts we want the viewer to recognize and learn about an underrepresented female in STEM, Hedy Lamarr, who laid the groundwork for a lot of the luxuries we have today such as WiFi, GPS, and Bluetooth."

Survey can be found here.
